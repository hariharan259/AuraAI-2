# AI Beauty Lab

## Overview

AI Beauty Lab is a multi-agent AI beauty intelligence platform that analyzes skin and hair health, recommends personalized routines, predicts outcomes, and tracks progress over time.

---

## Problem Statement

Most beauty applications provide generic recommendations without considering individual skin conditions, hair health, lifestyle factors, and long-term progress.

Users often struggle to identify effective routines and ingredients tailored to their specific needs.

---

## Solution

AI Beauty Lab combines computer vision, specialized AI agents, and beauty intelligence workflows to deliver personalized beauty guidance.

The system evaluates skin and hair conditions, generates customized routines, recommends ingredients, and forecasts improvement over time.
