# Judge Questions & Answers

## Q1. What problem does AuraAI solve?

Most beauty platforms provide generic recommendations.

AuraAI provides personalized skin and hair intelligence by analyzing individual conditions, lifestyle habits, and goals.

It helps users understand root causes instead of simply recommending products.

---

## Q2. Why did you choose this problem?

Millions of users struggle with trial-and-error beauty routines.

Recommendations are often generic and do not account for personal differences.

We wanted to create an intelligent beauty guidance system.

---

## Q3. Why use AI?

Traditional rule-based systems cannot effectively personalize recommendations.

AI can:

- Analyze multiple factors simultaneously
- Adapt recommendations
- Generate personalized routines
- Predict outcomes

---

## Q4. Why use a Multi-Agent Architecture?

Beauty intelligence requires multiple domains of expertise.

Instead of one general AI:

- Dermatologist Agent handles skin
- Trichologist Agent handles hair
- Ingredient Scientist handles ingredients
- Beauty Coach handles routines
- Outcome Predictor handles forecasting

This improves specialization and explainability.

---

## Q5. What makes AuraAI different from existing beauty apps?

Most apps:

- Analyze skin only
- Provide generic recommendations

AuraAI:

- Analyzes skin and hair
- Uses multiple AI agents
- Explains root causes
- Forecasts future outcomes
- Simulates lifestyle changes

---

## Q6. What is the most innovative feature?

Beauty Outcome Simulator.

- Routine consistency

and view projected beauty outcomes.

This helps users make informed decisions.
